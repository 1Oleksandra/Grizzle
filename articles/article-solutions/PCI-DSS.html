PCI DSS

Secure Your Payment Card Data

Protecting payment card information is critical for every business operating in the financial sector. PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) is an international standard established to ensure the security of payment card data. This standard imposes requirements that all organizations handling, storing, or transmitting payment card information must follow. Our PAM (Privileged Access Management) solutions play a crucial role in ensuring PCI DSS compliance, as privileged access management and security are key components of PCI DSS requirements.

Achieve PCI DSS Compliance with PAM Solutions

PCI DSS mandates strong security measures for systems processing payment card data. Our PAM solutions provide a comprehensive approach to ensuring PCI DSS compliance in the following areas:

  • Privileged Access Control
    PCI DSS requires that only authorized users have access to payment card data. PAM solutions centrally manage privileged access rights and allow only specific individuals to access critical data. This minimizes potential internal threats and misuse of payment card information.
  • Access Monitoring and Auditing
    PCI DSS mandates the logging of all access and the ability to track this data in the event of a security breach. PAM solutions continuously monitor and audit all privileged access activities. This ensures that every step is recorded, and enables a quick response if security breaches or suspicious activities are detected.
  • Secure Encryption and Authentication
    Payment card information and other sensitive data must be protected through encryption methods. PAM solutions enhance data security by limiting access to authorized users only, using strong authentication methods and encryption practices. Additionally, advanced security measures, such as multi-factor authentication (MFA), provide extra protection to ensure the security of passwords and credentials.
  • Mitigating Security Vulnerabilities
    PAM solutions help proactively identify security vulnerabilities in the system. This helps meet PCI DSS’s “security vulnerability remediation” requirement. Our PAM software ensures that the latest security patches are applied on systems where privileged accounts are accessed, minimizing security vulnerabilities.
  • Compliance and Reporting
    PCI DSS requires regular audits and reporting. PAM solutions provide comprehensive reporting to meet PCI DSS requirements. These reports make it easier to audit all access activities and security measures, ensuring organizations can demonstrate compliance during regulatory audits
