Just in-time-limited Privilege (JIT)

Temporary, Time-Limited Privileges provide temporary access rights to dedicated accounts for the duration of a specific task or project, allowing critical functions to be carried out. This approach enables users to gain privileged access only for the necessary duration without the need for permanently high-privilege accounts.

Users hold these privileges for a predetermined time period, and their access is automatically revoked once the time expires. This mechanism minimizes privilege escalation and permanent security vulnerabilities by granting access only for the necessary time.

Implementing time-limited privileges not only increases operational flexibility but also strengthens security protocols, thereby ensuring the protection of systems and data.
