Privileged Account Session Management

Management of Privileged Account Sessions is a security mechanism developed to provide complete control and oversight over the operations carried out in the organization's most critical systems. This module specifically manages the monitoring and recording process of privileged accesses, which pose high risks. System administrators can monitoractivities occurring throughout all privileged sessions in real time and can access detailed transaction records by reverting to past sessions if necessary.

This process goes beyond ensuring system security and creates a proactive security layer against cyber threats. Recording privileged sessions enables the early detection of potential breaches and accelerates incident response. This allows security teams to respond more effectively and promptly. Additionally, the detailed logs and session records enable the examination of all account activities during audit processes, contributing to the complete fulfillment of compliance requirements.

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Administrators can live monitor all sessions of users with privileged access to critical systems and intervene when necessary.
  • Detailed Record Keeping: Every transaction carried out with privileged accounts is recorded throughout the session. These records can be used in post-incident investigations or in analyzing security breaches.
  • Transparency and Accountability: Monitoring privileged sessions transparently reveals what types of actions users are performing in the systems and ensures accountability.
  • Facilitating Incident Response: Quick access to detailed session records allows security teams to act faster in the event of a breach or potential threat, improving incident response processes.
  • Meeting Compliance Requirements: Many regulations and standards mandate the monitoring and auditing of critical accounts. Privileged session management addresses such compliance requirements, reducing the organization's risks.
